# SIM & Connectivity

## Tajikistan — Tcell or Megafon
- Get SIM at **DYU airport on arrival** (kiosks open for late-night flights)
- Passport required for registration (a 5-min process)
- Cost: ~50–80 TJS (~$5–8) for a tourist plan with 10–30 GB
- **Megafon** has the broadest coverage; **Tcell** is comparable in cities
- Coverage **drops sharply** in Fann Mountains valleys past Penjikent — usable in Artush village, **nonexistent on the trek** past the alplager

## Uzbekistan — Ucell or Beeline
- Get SIM at TAS airport or any kiosk in Samarkand/Tashkent
- Passport required
- Cost: ~50,000 UZS (~$4) for ~30 GB tourist plan
- **Beeline** has the best 4G coverage
- Both work fine in all the cities we visit

## eSIM alternative — recommended
- **Airalo "Asialink"** (regional Central Asia) — works in both UZ and TJ
- Cost: ~$10–20 for 5GB / 30 days
- Buy/activate before you leave Denver
- No registration hassle, no SIM-swap on arrival
- Trade-off: a physical local SIM gives you a local number for guesthouse calls and Yandex Go

> [!tip] Use both
> Activate eSIM for instant data on landing. Buy a physical Tcell SIM in Dushanbe for the local number (Yandex, calling guesthouses, calling driver). Costs ~$10 total.

## VPN — required for some services
- Several services are intermittently blocked in Uzbekistan (some Western news, Skype, occasionally messaging)
- Install **Proton VPN** or **Mullvad** before leaving
- VPN also handles US-based banking apps that geofence Central Asia
- Trekking note: VPN doesn't help if there's no signal at all

## Connectivity by location

| Location | Coverage | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Dushanbe | 4G, reliable | Use Tcell/Megafon |
| Anzob Pass / road to Penjikent | Patchy 3G | Drops in tunnels |
| Penjikent town | 4G | Reliable |
| [[Artush and Pasrud]] villages | 2G/3G | Marginal, expect drops |
| Fann Mountains (trek) | **None above ~2500m** | InReach territory |
| Samarkand | 4G/5G | UZ SIM strong |
| Tashkent | 4G/5G | UZ SIM strong |
| Train Samarkand → Tashkent | 4G most of route | OK for music streaming |

## Garmin inReach or PLB
> [!warning] Trek = no cell coverage
> Above the alplagers in the [[Kulikalon-Alaudin Loop]], there is **no cell coverage at all** for 4 of 5 trek days. Carry a Garmin inReach Mini 2 (or equivalent) with active subscription. Test it before flying. Share tracking link with one stateside contact.
