# Itinerary Overview — 14-day skeleton, TJ first

> [!info] Booked vs. plan
> Booking gives us **16 calendar days** (DYU 01:00 Oct 3 → TAS 09:35 Oct 18). The 14-day skeleton below leaves **2 buffer days** — slot them as a Hissar Fortress half-day (Day 2.5), an extra Samarkand night, or a contingency for weather/border/GI delays. Don't burn them up front.

> [!tip] Why TJ-first
> October Fann is at the edge of season. Trekking Days 4–8 (Oct 6–10) catches the **last reliable weather window** before snow starts closing high passes. UZ in mid-October is in its **best window** — 15–25°C days, harvest in the bazaars, fewer crowds. The reverse order would put the trek in late October, gambling on snow.

## Day-by-day

### Tajikistan phase

**Day 1 — Sat Oct 3 — [[Dushanbe]]** (sleep: Dushanbe)
Land DYU 01:00. Sleep in. Light wander Rudaki Park, lunch nearby, finalize trek logistics.

**Day 2 — Sun Oct 4 — [[Dushanbe]]** (sleep: Dushanbe)
National Museum of Antiquities (Sleeping Buddha), Mehrgon Bazaar, dinner at a qurutob place. Hit ATM for trek cash.

**Day 3 — Mon Oct 5 — DYU → [[Penjikent]]** (sleep: Penjikent)
Shared taxi over **Anzob Pass (3372m)** — 5–6h, scenic mountain road. Arrive Penjikent afternoon. Sogdian ruins if time.

**Day 4 — Tue Oct 6 — Penjikent → [[Artush and Pasrud]]** (sleep: Artuch alplager or camp)
Provision food at Penjikent bazaar AM. Drive to Artuch Alplager (~2200m). Meet horseman, leg-stretcher walk up-valley before dinner.

**Day 5 — Wed Oct 7 — Trek Day 1** (sleep: Kulikalon camp)
Chukurak Valley → Chukurak Pass (3180m) → Kulikalon Lakes (~2800m).

**Day 6 — Thu Oct 8 — Trek Day 2** (sleep: Kulikalon camp)
Rest / exploration day around Kulikalon Lakes. Side hike to Mutnoe or upper basins.

**Day 7 — Fri Oct 9 — Trek Day 3** (sleep: Alaudin Lakes camp)
**Alaudin Pass (3860m)** crossing → Alaudin Lakes (~2800m). High day.

**Day 8 — Sat Oct 10 — Trek Day 4** (sleep: Alaudin Lakes camp)
Alaudin Lakes day — explore upper basin, optional side hike to Mutnye Lakes.

**Day 9 — Sun Oct 11 — Trek Day 5 + transit** (sleep: [[Samarkand]])
Trek out to road head → drive to Penjikent → cross **Sarazm/Jartepa border** → Samarkand. Long day, ~10–12h door-to-door.

### Uzbekistan phase

**Day 10 — Mon Oct 12 — [[Samarkand]]** (sleep: Samarkand)
Decompress. **Shah-i-Zinda at sunrise** (alley empty, golden light). Bibi-Khanym, Siyob Bazaar lunch, Gur-e-Amir, sunset at Registan.

**Day 11 — Tue Oct 13 — [[Samarkand]]** (sleep: Samarkand)
Ulugh Beg Observatory, Afrosiyob Museum, slow afternoon. Sunset blue hour at Registan from across the plaza.

**Day 12 — Wed Oct 14 — SAM → [[Tashkent]]** (sleep: Tashkent)
**Afrosiyob train** ~2h 10m. Afternoon Chorsu Bazaar, evening relax.

**Day 13 — Thu Oct 15 — [[Tashkent]]** (sleep: Tashkent)
**Khast Imam** complex AM, **Plov Center (Besh Qozon)** lunch — get there before 12:00 to see cooking, eat ~12:30. Afternoon metro architecture tour.

**Day 14 — Fri Oct 16 — [[Tashkent]]** (sleep: Tashkent)
Buffer / Soviet brutalism walk / repack / last bazaar / dinner.

**(Buffer) — Sat Oct 17** (sleep: Tashkent)
Slot for delay recovery or extra Samarkand/Tashkent day.

**Sun Oct 18 — DEPART**
Leave hotel 06:00. TAS 09:35 → IST → DEN 18:25.

## What we're NOT doing (parked)

> [!warning] Bukhara + Khiva
> Don't fit a 14-day skeleton with a 5-day trek. To add Bukhara would require: drop trek Day 8 (rest day), drop Dushanbe Day 2, run a 4-day trek instead, fit Bukhara as 2 nights between Samarkand and Tashkent.
>
> **Adjusted Bukhara-included skeleton:**
> - Day 1 Dushanbe arrive · Day 2 Dushanbe → Penjikent (skip Hissar) · Days 3 Penjikent + provision · Days 4–7 **4-day trek** (Kulikalon-Alaudin shortened, skip rest day) · Day 8 trek out → Samarkand · Day 9–10 Samarkand · Day 11–12 Bukhara · Day 13 Bukhara → Tashkent · Day 14 Tashkent
>
> Trade-off: thinner trek, no rest day in Fann, skipping the Mehrgon Bazaar day in Dushanbe. Decide as a pair.

> [!warning] Pamir Highway
> Out — see [[Kulikalon-Alaudin Loop]] and Index parked section. Pairs better with a Kyrgyz exit next time.

## Flex levers

If we lose a day to weather/border/GI:
- **Cut Samarkand Day 11** (Ulugh Beg + Afrosiyob Museum compressed into Day 10 afternoon)
- **Cut Tashkent Day 14** (combined buffer + last day)

If we get an extra day:
- **Hissar Fortress** half-day from Dushanbe (Day 2.5)
- **Iskanderkul day trip** from Dushanbe or as trek prologue (Day 4 alternate)
- **Shahrisabz** day trip from Samarkand (Tamerlane's birthplace, ~80km south)
