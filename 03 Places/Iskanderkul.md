# Iskanderkul

Largest lake in the Fann Mountains at 2195m, ~130km NE of Dushanbe. "Alexander's Lake" — named for Alexander the Great, who supposedly passed through here. Turquoise water, Fann panorama, accessible by 4x4 road from the south (Anzob road) side. **Not part of the [[Kulikalon-Alaudin Loop]]** — different Fann valley system.

`role:: optional day-trip / float-day option`
`days_here:: 0-1`

## When to consider it
- **Day-trip from Dushanbe** if we have a buffer day (~10–11h round trip)
- **Trek prologue** — Day 3 could route via Iskanderkul instead of straight to Penjikent, but adds ~4h and complicates trek logistics
- **Trek finish point alternative** — if we exit east instead of west, but doesn't align with Penjikent→border plan

> [!warning] Not on the default plan
> The 14-day skeleton goes Dushanbe → Penjikent → Artuch (west). Iskanderkul is in the east of the Fann complex. Adding it means dropping something else from the day plan or burning a buffer day.

## What's there

### The lake itself
- 2195m, ~3.4 km² surface, turquoise from glacial melt
- Snake Lake nearby — small detour
- **Fann Niagara** waterfall — 40m drop, ~30 min hike from main parking
- Chaikhana (tea house) by the shore — tea, beer, simple Tajik meals for ~30 TJS

### Things to do
- Walk the shore (~1 hour casual)
- Hike to Fann Niagara (30–45 min one way)
- Dip your feet (water is cold — glacial)
- Photograph Fann peaks reflected in lake at sunrise/sunset

## Logistics

### Day trip from Dushanbe
- Distance: 130 km
- Time: 3–4h drive each way (4WD recommended, last section is dirt + rough)
- Tour operators offer day tours for ~$100–150 per vehicle (split)
- Self-drive: not advised unless familiar
- **Best season: May–October** — we're at the edge in mid-October but lake is open

### If we slot in (Day 17 buffer or earlier)
- Leave Dushanbe 07:00
- Lake by 10:30–11:00
- Lunch at chaikhana
- Hike to Fann Niagara
- Depart 15:00, return Dushanbe 18:30–19:00

## Sleeping near the lake
There's a Soviet-era turbaza (basic guesthouse) at the lake with cabins for ~$15–25. **Cold in October, unheated cabins**. Better to day-trip from Dushanbe.
